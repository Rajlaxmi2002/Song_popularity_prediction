Song Popularity Prediction

Predict whether a song will be popular or not

Data

Data description

Dataset for training the model was from a kaggle competition. Features to describe a particular song were:

  • id: int64 - Identifier for the song
  • song_duration_ms: float64 - Duration of the song in milliseconds
  • acousticness: float64 - A confidence measure of whether the track is acoustic
  • danceability: float64 - Describes how suitable a track is for dancing based on a combination of musical elements
  • energy: float64 - Represents a perceptual measure of intensity and activity
  • instrumentalness: float64 - Predicts whether a track contains no vocals
  • key: float64 - The key the track is in
  • liveness: float64 - Detects the presence of an audience in the recording
  • loudness: float64 - Overall loudness of the track in decibels
  • audio_mode: int64 - Modality of the track
  • speechiness: float64 - Speechiness detects the presence of spoken words in a track
  • tempo: float64 - The overall estimated tempo of a track in beats per minute
  • time_signature: int64 - An estimated overall time signature of a track
  • audio_valence: float64 - A measure from 0.0 to 1.0 describing the musical positiveness conveyed by a track
  • song_popularity: int64 - Popularity of the song (e.g., number of plays or likes)

Model

Model used: AdaBoost classifier

Hyperparameters

  • Base estimator : Decision Tree classifier
  • learning_rate : 0.5
  • n_estimators : 90
  • max_depth : 14

Results

Accuracy : 0.6421

Specificity : 0.6450
